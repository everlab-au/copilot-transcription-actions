Allow Microphone Access

The extension needs access to your microphone to record audio for transcription.

Why does this extension need microphone access?

This extension records audio from your microphone for transcription purposes. Your audio data is processed locally and never sent to any server without your explicit permission.

Having trouble?

If the button above doesn't work, you can also enable permissions manually:

  1. Click on the lock icon in your address bar
  2. Select "Site settings" or "Permissions"
  3. Find "Microphone" and change the setting to "Allow"
  4. Reload this page
